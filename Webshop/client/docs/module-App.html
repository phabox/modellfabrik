App

Main component of the webshop. This view bundles all other views as subcomponents. The PageHeader component with navigation to the other views is always shown. Based on the selected route the corresponding Router-View is displayed. Since this is the central component of the wbeshop which is always alive, it is also responsible of fetching and listening to data from the backend-server. This way we avoid fetching and subscribing data too often when subcomponents are destroyed and recreated based on the route.
